Important Information about your Pick-up Point

How the pick-up process works

Before we start picking up passengers, our guides prepare the vehicles and print out a list of the customers that have requested pick-up services. The pick-up process changes every time, depending on the number of passengers and their location. We try to minimize the waiting time for your pick-up, but it can take up to 30 minutes, so please be patient while you wait for your guide to reach you.

The bus stops look like this

Still, It is very important to be ready at your pick-up location at the start of the pick-up time, so that you don‘t miss your pick-up.
Please read your voucher carefully before pickup and be aware of who is operating your tour. There can be different labels for different tours and different operators. The vehicles are prominently marked with the operator's logo, so you should be able to spot them easily.

Look for the marked pickup vehicles. Markings are tour and operator dependent, read your voucher carefully.

Pick-ups in central Reykjavik

We offer pick-up services all around Reykjavik but, due to rules set by the city, buses are not allowed to pick up passengers up at their hotels in the center of Reykjavik, and must use designated pick-up points.
The pick-up points are marked with blue columns, so they are hard to miss.
Take a look at the map. If your hotel is inside the blue area, we kindly ask you to select one of the bus stops marked there as a pick-up location.
